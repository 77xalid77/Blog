Ter Stegen

Marc-André ter Stegen (German pronunciation: [ˌmaʁk ʔanˈdʁeː teːɐ̯ ˈsteːɡn̩] ⓘ;[2] born 30 April 1992) is a German professional footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for La Liga club Barcelona, which he captains, and the Germany national team. Considered one of the best goalkeepers in the world, he is known for his reflexes, passing, and ball-playing ability.[3][9] After four seasons in the Bundesliga with Borussia Mönchengladbach, making 108 league appearances, he joined Barcelona for €12 million in 2014. He won the treble in his first season in Spain, playing for Barcelona in Copa del Rey and UEFA Champions League.
